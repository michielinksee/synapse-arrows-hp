About

About Synapse Arrows

Designing decision intelligence for the AI era — a Singapore-based technology company

Mission

From Delivering Information to Evolving Decision-Making

As AI search rapidly proliferates, businesses need more than just ranking high on Google — they need to be accurately cited by AI. Synapse Arrows combines deep expertise in AEO (AI Engine Optimization) with hands-on experience from building our own products to tackle this new challenge.

Based in Singapore, we occupy a unique position bridging Southeast Asia and Japan. We operate through two pillars: proprietary AI product development and consulting. We offer 7 AI products and 4 consulting services.

Vision

Three Phases of Evolution

Phase 1

Human-guided AI

Humans lead, AI assists. Label scan to AI explanation, manual bookkeeping to AI classification. Most of our current products are at this phase.

Phase 2

Agent-augmented

AI agents autonomously execute tasks while humans supervise. Bantou's automated bookkeeping and KanseiLINK's auto-rating updates are transitioning to this phase.

Phase 3

Agent-autonomous

Multiple AI agents collaborate while humans focus solely on decision-making. Linksee Memory's cross-agent memory sharing lays the foundation for this phase.
